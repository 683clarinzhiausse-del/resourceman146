# ResourceMan - Vision and Mission

## Vision
To become the premier digital ecosystem that bridges traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation, empowering local industries and individual entrepreneurs through a transparent, high-performance commerce and management platform.

## Mission
ResourceMan is dedicated to providing an accessible and secure marketplace for premium footwear and technology. We strive to:
- Support and promote the **Marikina shoe industry**.
- Provide scalable opportunities for **resellers** via tier-based incentives.
- Ensure operational excellence through **integrated payroll** and **AI-driven support**.
- Deliver an exceptional, secure experience for every customer.
